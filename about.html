About us

Company Profile

Formed in 2001, Creative Team Venture (CTV) is a civil construction & interior Decoration studio providing high level of service in the residential and commercial industry. Our team takes pride in creating luxurious, creative, functional and innovative space design.

Each project starts with a personalized concept that directly correlates to these needs of our clients and their individual style. We rely on our professional knowledge and advanced experience in the design field to walk our clients through the process of interior designing, modeling and construction.

Vision

To provide value-added construction services to our customers by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the construction process and beyond

Mission

Our mission is to be a dynamic partner of our client, in providing the highest quality construction and allied services, exceeding our customers' expectations in a safe environment.

What we do?

When our onsite technical team executes projects as per Architects/PMC's design and drawings under their guidelines to make our clients dream into reality, Our Quantity Surveying team helps our architects and PMCs in formulating BOQs, Specifications, Tender Documents, On site vendor Bill certification etc.,

We also take up Developmental works like, layout formation/development, Rain Water Harvesting, Construction of water bodies like, Under Ground Sump, Water Treatment Plants, Swimming Pools etc.,

As our tag line says "WE ARE YOUR CONSTRUCTION PARTNERS, NOT JUST CONTRACTORS", we partner with our architects/PMCs and run the project right from tender formulation to material finalization to material sourcing to special vendor finalization to Housekeeping as their team and handover the project to our client with well qualified and experienced
